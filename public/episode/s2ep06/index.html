3Speak has made a post detailing an overview of their new Acela Core. I’m assuming the image in the post is meant to try to make it more simpler to see the overview. To us this looks like added points of failure, massive development and constant ongoing maintenance. Something that will require a lot of overhead to operate. Not to mention how many points of attack vectors will this open up?
