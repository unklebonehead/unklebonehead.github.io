CastGarden is now on #Lokinet!

Lokinet is similar to Tor only it works at the network layer and the result is you can use any browser and many desktop apps anonymously.
More about Lokinet - <a href="https://lokinet.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://lokinet.org/</a>

CastGarden is Peertube instance that utilizes the Hive-Tube plugin for podcasters and to monetize their content with the Hive blockchain. It has Podcasting 2.0 capabilities, streams in P2P and is also on #Lokinet.
If you are a podcaster and interested in syndicating/monetizing your content on it the contact info is on their about page.
