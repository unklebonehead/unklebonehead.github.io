We had an issue where I (Unkle Bonehead) lost the keys for the Defluenced Hive account.
<!--more-->
 I ended up having to start a new one. So make sure you are following DefluencedCast.

#### **Good Ideas**

- Back them up Back them up again in a different place. 
- Back them up a third time in yet another place. 
- Print them out on paper. 
- Put them in a safe. 
- Send a copy to a trusted friend or family member. 
- Repeat as many times as you feel necessary.

#### **Bad ideas**

- Send them in unencrypted email to the FBI, CIA or any other 3 letter agency. 
- Post it notes on your monitor Bulletin board at your church, the library, or other public place. 
- On hard drives that have a history of failing. 
- Let your browser remember them Open forums and message boards. 
- Tattoo on your forearm. 
- On your dog’s ID collar. 
- On a refrigerator magnet in your RV. 
- Let Coinbase, Kraken, Binance, or another exchange hold your keys 

>NOT your keys, NOT your crypto!
